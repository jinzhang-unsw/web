Biography

Jin Zhang is currently pursuing Postgraduate Research(PhD) in Wireless Networking domain under the supervision of Salil Kanhere and Wen Hu, in the School of Computer Science and Engineering at the University of New South Wales, Sydney, Australia. He received his B.S. degree (2012) in Communication Engineering of Northwestern Polytechnic University, P.R. China. He is a member of Network Research Laboratory in the CSE at UNSW. His research interests include Wireless Sensor Network, Internet of Things, Embedded System. Jin has joined NRL since August 2013.
