This website present the WiFi Channel State Information (CSI) dataset which contains 45 types of human activities conducted by 61 individuals.

Paper

Act-ID: Human Identification Through Activities Using WiFi signals” to be published in 2020

Dataset

We recruited 61 volunteers who were both male and female and aged between 12 and 52 years. Each subject was asked to perform 3 categories, 45 types of activities. every single type of activity was conducted for once. Each activity lasts around 5 seconds. The activities that are inherently of finite duration would be repeated in limited times. The whole data collection experiment lasts over 20 days.
